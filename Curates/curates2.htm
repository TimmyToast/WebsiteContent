TOAST Curates #1

GATHER COOK FEAST

30th March, 6-8pm
TOAST Westbourne Grove

Join TOAST founder Jessica Seaton to celebrate the publication of her new book ‘Gather Cook Feast’.

Full of simple, seasonal and nourishing recipes, Gather Cook Feast explores the connection between the food that we eat and the land on which we live. We will be holding a Q&A with Jessica and her editor, Juliet Annan, which will be followed by a book signing – and a celebration with food and drink from the book.

‘Beautiful recipes deeply rooted in time and place – my favourite sort of food. Certain to become often used in my kitchen.’ Anna Jones

‘Each dish is a celebration of the fruit of the land, both grown and foraged. For the dreamers and ramblers among us.’ Guardian

Entry for this event is now closed.
