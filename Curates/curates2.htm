TOAST Curates #2

BLOCK PRINTING

27th April, 6-8pm
TOAST Bath

Block printing is a centuries-old craft and – despite the advances in technology – still persists in many workshops across India. Wood blocks are hand carved, dipped in dye and lined up by eye on top of the cloth, resulting in a lovely uneven print. It is these small (and beautiful) irregularities in colour and design which speak the making of the human hand and make each piece unique.

Our spring and summer collections include many block printed pieces and to celebrate the technique we would like to invite you to an evening of block printing in our Bath store, led by textile designer Ren Valuzyte.

Explore the design processes behind this technique and experiment with block printing. A limited number of places are available. If you would like to attend this event please register below.
