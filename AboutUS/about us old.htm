• ABOUT US

    Toast designs and retails clothes and homewares for modern living. We endeavour to create products that are unique - designed with great care, made of exceptional materials - and to present them in simple and inspiring ways.

    Established in West Wales in 1997 by Jessica and Jamie Seaton, Toast has grown steadily to become a company renowned for its uncompromising commitment to quality, creativity and thoughtfulness. With a large online following, 11 shops throughout the UK and offices in both London and Swansea, Toast today is a fast growing, forward looking company that never loses sight of the core values on which it was founded.

    Toast's collections for women and home are designed and developed in-house by a dedicated design team, and manufactured by trusted suppliers around the world. Our designs evolve with each season, but every collection is informed by four words:

    Simplicity. Modernity. Comfort. Colour
